Meaning of The Farming

farming:- is the practice of growing crops or raising animals for food. This includes everything from planting and harvesting crops to caring for animals and selling or distributing the resulting of food products. Farming is an essential part of our food system, and it has been practiced in some form or another for thousands of years. There are many different types of farming, including crop farming, livestock farming, and aquaculture.
